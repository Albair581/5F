Learn How to Acquire the App! (Mobile)

iOS Safari / iOS Chrome

In your browser, open this website and tap on the   icon located at the top right corner,   scroll down the list of options, then tap "Add to Home Screen".  If you don’t see Add to Home Screen, you can add it. Scroll down to the bottom of the list, tap Edit Actions, then tap "  Add to Home Screen".


iOS Firefox

In Firefox, open this website, but this time, the    icon is located at the top left corner. The rest remains the same as Safari.


Android Chrome

Don't worry -- we gotchu. Go to the settings and simply click install. Android made our life and your life easier.
But if you REALLY want to try it out yourself, here are the instructions from Google: Instructions from Google


Other Mobile Browsers

Find the share icon and then add this website to your home screen.


Learn How to Acquire the App! (Mac)

Safari (Mac)

In Safari, open this website and tap on the   icon located at the top right corner,   scroll down the list of options, then tap "Add to Dock".


Learn How to Acquire the App! (Windows)

Edge (Windows)

In edge, open this website, find the 3 dots (...) on the top right corner of the browser. Find the apps section and then choose "Install this site as an app".

如何取得應用程式 (行動裝置)

iOS Safari / iOS Chrome

在指定瀏覽器中，打開此網站，然後點擊右上方的   圖標，  往下捲動，找到 "加入主畫面".  如果沒有看到，可以手動新增。捲動到最底部，編輯動作，然後選擇"  加入主畫面".


iOS 火狐

在火狐瀏覽器，打開此網站，但是這次要點擊左上方的    圖標。其餘和 iOS Safari 相同。


安卓 Chrome

不要擔心 -- 我們已經為你準備好了。到設定並簡單地點選安裝。安卓讓我們和你更加方便。
如果你很想要自己嘗試看看，可以參考 Google 的指示: 前往網站


其他行動裝置瀏覽器

試著找到分享網站的圖標，然後將此網站加入主畫面。
